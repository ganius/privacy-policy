Privacy Policy for Sudoku Love

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Sudoku Love ("the App") is developed by Gani Simsek. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Data Collection

Sudoku Love does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your game progress and best times locally on your device using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
